What can we offer you?

12 Billion Years and Counting

STS has an unmatched array of options for customers to choose from when selecting their stars. Between the finest selection of pre-existing stars, to stars custom-made just for you, there will always be a choice for you!
Here are just a few options available to you as an STS customer:

  • Choose from a near infinite amount of pre-existing, unclaimed stars
  • Find stars of all types and sizes, from brand new stars to long dead ones
  • Request special colors for your stars (don't worry about how that works, just trust us)
  • Select special shipping instructions and express shipping to have your order delivered just the way you want

Pricing Information

Generally speaking, pre-made stars will be cheaper than custom-made, though prices may vary depending on availability. In addition, older stars will usually be more expensive than newer ones (unless they are pre-made white or black dwarfs, which are usually left-over stock), and larger stars will also be more expensive than smaller ones.
